Grading Policy

Assessment split for ES242(N): exams, CodeChef assignment contests, regular practice, diagnostic test, and attendance bonus.

Total

100 marks + up to 3 attendance bonus marks.

Download grading policy PDF

50

Exams

Exam I: 25 marks. Exam II: 25 marks.

32

Assignment contests on CodeChef

Six CodeChef Starters will be considered. Best four performances count, 8 marks each. Indicative scoring: 4+ solved = 8, 3 solved = 7, 2 solved = 5, 1 solved = 3, participation with no solved problem = 1. Scoring may be adjusted based on contest difficulty.

15

Regular practice

Participation on CodeChef, Codeforces, LeetCode, and AtCoder. Each contest participation with at least one accepted submission earns 1 mark, capped at 15. Counting window: 11 May - 30 June.

03

Diagnostic test

Beginning-of-course diagnostic test. Marks are awarded for attempting/attending the test, not performance.

+3

Attendance bonus

Attendance is outside the regular 100 marks. Students with at least 30% attendance are eligible; 30%, 60%, and 90% attendance correspond to 1, 2, and 3 bonus marks respectively. Applied only if it improves the final letter grade.
